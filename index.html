About Me

Hello perspective employers. I am Logan Ayers I was born in Georgia, but have lived in various locations, both domestic and foreign. From Connecticut, to Ohio, to Texas, to England, I have done my fair share of traversing the North Western hemisphere. I have lived in Kansas for 5 years now.

I started coding in highschool, starting with relatively easy things, like coding a card deck that shuffles itself or recreating Space Invader in Python. I attended the coding bootcamp at KU where I expanded my repetoire, learning full stack development.

Coding in general has been a fulfilling past time. I enjoy analyzing a problem and figuring out viable solutions, in the future I plan on making my hobby into a career.
